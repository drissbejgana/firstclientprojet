Leviathan BuyBack

  • 6% buyback tax is applied to each transaction; converted into BNB and stored inside the Leviathan contract.

  • All the tokens purchased via buybacks are instantly sent to the burn address and the BNB used for purchase is deposited into the locked liquidity pool.
